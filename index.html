About

I am a postdoc in physics at Aarhus University working in the ion trap group of Prof. Michael Drewsen. My current research activities involve state preparation and precision spectroscopy of cold molecular ions as part of the COMIQ network. Previously, I was a graduate student in Mike Chapman's lab at Georgia Tech working with trapped Th3+ and Ba+ ions. More details on my research can be found here.

In my spare time, I enjoy photography, various outdoor activities, reading, and working on various projects related to Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and open source software. Most of my projects can be found on GitHub.
