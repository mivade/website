About

I am a physics postdoc at Aarhus University working in the ion trap group of Prof. Michael Drewsen. My current research activities involve state preparation and precision spectroscopy of cold molecular ions as part of the COMIQ network. Previously, I was a graduate student in Mike Chapman's lab at Georgia Tech working with trapped Th3+ and Ba+ ions. More details on my research can be found here.

In my spare time, I enjoy photography, playing soccer and basketball, bicycling, reading, and working on various projects related to Arduino, Raspbery Pi, and open source software.
